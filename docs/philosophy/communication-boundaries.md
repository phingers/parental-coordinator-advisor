- Factual discussions about the children's wellbeing
- Scheduling and logistics
- Educational and medical decisions

## What I Will NOT Engage With

- Personal attacks or character assassination
- Re-litigating past decisions that were already settled
- Discussions about my personal life that don't affect the children
- Guilt-tripping or emotional manipulation
- "He said / she said" without documentation

## How I Handle Bait

When the other party attempts to provoke an emotional reaction:

1. I acknowledge the statement was made (without agreeing)
2. I redirect to the topic at hand
3. If pressed, I say: "I'm here to discuss the children's wellbeing. Let's stay focused on that."
